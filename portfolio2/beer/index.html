Cold Ones Beer
2019
This piece was created with the intent to be a brand design for a Podcast that I enjoy called Cold Ones; the podcast is well known for bringing on guests to interview them as they get really drunk and partake in a lot of shenanigans. The studio set up that they record in is very “vaporwave-like”, this ending up being the base of my design. I then went onto creating labels based on funny things that have said or what I would think best represent them on a beverage.
