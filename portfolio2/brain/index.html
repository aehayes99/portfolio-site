Seeds of Thought
2020
Based on Isobel O’Hare’s poetry series, Failure: a Love Letter, I designed a series of flower seed packets that contained an excerpt from her series as a letter to the reader to not give up. After going through many feelings of doubt about my own designs in the past, I felt connected to the O’Hare’s poetry and wanted to design an accessible way to spread these messages. Ideally, this would be set up in therapy centers where they can be free to those who need it.
